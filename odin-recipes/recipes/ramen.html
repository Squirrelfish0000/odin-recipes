Ramen

Description

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup known for its rich, savory broth and springy noodles. It is commonly served with toppings such as sliced meat, soft-boiled eggs, and vegetables.

This dish is comforting, flavorful, and highly customizable. Whether you prefer a light soy-based broth or a creamy tonkotsu base, ramen is a satisfying meal enjoyed all around the world.

Ingredients

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 500ml chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • 100g sliced chicken or pork
  • Green onions
  • Spinach or bok choy
  • Soy sauce
  • Miso paste (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the broth in a pot and season with soy sauce and miso paste.
  3. Place the cooked noodles into a bowl.
  4. Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
  5. Add sliced meat, eggs, and vegetables on top.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
